About SkyVidya Workshop

Bringing Aviation into the Classroom

SkyVidya is an aviation-focused hands-on workshop where students build a functional balsa wood glider and learn the core principles of flight — lift, drag, thrust, and aerodynamics. Perfect for sparking curiosity in aerospace & engineering.

4 Day Program

Your Journey to the Skies

DAY 1

Introduction to Aviation

Flight history, basic aerodynamics (lift, drag), and aircraft components. Interactive demonstrations.

DAY 2

Design & Materials

Understanding wing shapes, fuselage, tail. Start cutting and shaping glider parts.

DAY 3

Assembly & Balancing

Complete glider assembly, attaching wings & tail, weight balancing, structural integrity check.

DAY 4

Flight Day & Awards

Outdoor flight tests, trim adjustments, mini competition, quiz, certificate distribution.

About SkyVidya

Built on a Mission to Inspire

SkyVidya was founded to bring real-world aviation experiences to school students. We believe every child deserves to experience the magic of flight and the confidence that comes from building something with their own hands.

Based in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, we partner with schools across the region to deliver structured, fun, and educational aeromodelling workshops.

📌 MSME Registered Enterprise — SkyVidya is an independent initiative. Workshops are conducted by certified trainers with school collaboration.
